About College

Welcome to Government College of Engineering, Kolhapur - an institution at the forefront of innovation, established in 2023 with a vision to redefine engineering education. Dedicated to excellence, our institution proudly offers five cutting-edge programs designed to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Our programs include Computer Science and Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Mechanical and Automation Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. With a commitment to nurturing talent and fostering innovation, each program combines a comprehensive curriculum with hands-on learning experiences. Our state-of-the-art facilities, coupled with a faculty of accomplished educators and industry experts, ensure that students receive a holistic education that prepares them to excel in their chosen fields. To maintain a focus on quality education and personalized attention, each program accommodates a maximum capacity of 60 students. This allows us to create an intimate learning environment where students receive individualized guidance and mentorship, fostering their growth as future leaders and innovators. Join us at Government College of Engineering, Kolhapur, where knowledge meets opportunity, and where the next generation of engineers and technologists are empowered to shape the future. Come, be a part of our journey as we pave the way for innovation and excellence in engineering education! Warm regards.
